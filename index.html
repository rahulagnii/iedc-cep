About Us

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) is an active student-run cell under CEP that seeks to create and promote innovation and entrepreneurship skills among the students of CEP. The cell works in close association with Kerala Startup Mission, CEP and other student clubs. It envisions fostering innovative thinking and becoming a facilitator between the students inside the campus and the industry outside by acting as a hub of contacts and network that helps in finding the wannabe entrepreneurs the right people to approach for any task.
